# Hazard Signals Guidance (Synthetic)
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Version:** v0.1-synthetic

## 1. Purpose
This document maps external hazard signals (scores, zones, proximity) to underwriting actions.

## 2. Wildfire
### 2.1 Wildfire Score Bands
- **Low**: No action required.
- **Moderate**: **SHOULD** recommend mitigation checklist.
- **High**: **SHALL** refer; **MUST** request defensible space evidence.
- **Severe**: **MUST** decline unless mitigation documentation exists and underwriter approves.

### 2.2 Evidence Requirements
For High/Severe:
- photo evidence of clearance
- roof material type (tile/metal/asphalt)
- distance to nearest wildland boundary (if available)

## 3. Flood
### 3.1 SFHA Handling
If in SFHA:
- submission **SHALL** refer
- applicant **MUST** confirm flood insurance status
- **SHOULD** recommend water backup coverage where eligible

### 3.2 Water Proximity Heuristic (Non-SFHA)
If distance to major water body < 0.5 miles:
- **SHOULD** ask about prior seepage/foundation water intrusion
- **MAY** recommend higher deductible or water mitigation devices

## 4. Wind / Hail
- If wind score High: **SHOULD** request roof shape/type and last inspection date.
- If hail score High: **MAY** recommend impact-resistant roof endorsement where supported.

## 5. Earthquake (CA demo)
- Earthquake risk does not change base eligibility in this demo.
- **MAY** recommend earthquake endorsement if product supports it.
