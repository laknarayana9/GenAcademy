# Homeowners Underwriting Guidelines (Synthetic)
**Carrier:** DemoCarrier  
**Product:** HO3 / HO5  
**State:** CA (demo)  
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01  
**Version:** v0.1-synthetic

## 1. Eligibility Overview
### 1.1 Eligible Risk Types
- The risk **MUST** be a 1–4 family residential dwelling used as **primary residence** or **seasonal residence**.
- The dwelling **MUST NOT** be used for commercial operations beyond incidental home office (see §1.4).
- The insured **MUST** have an insurable interest.

### 1.2 Ineligible (Hard Declines)
- The risk **MUST BE DECLINED** if **vacant > 30 consecutive days** at time of inception.
- The risk **MUST BE DECLINED** if the dwelling has **prior fire loss within 36 months** and no documented mitigation.
- The risk **MUST BE DECLINED** if **knob-and-tube wiring is present** and not remediated.

### 1.3 Protection Class (Fire Protection)
- If protection class is **9 or 10**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED** unless:
  - distance to fire station ≤ 10 miles **AND**
  - distance to hydrant ≤ 1,000 feet.

### 1.4 Home Business / Short-Term Rental
- If the property is used for **short-term rental**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- A home office with ≤ 1 employee and no customer foot traffic **MAY** be acceptable.

## 2. Construction & Maintenance Standards
### 2.1 Roof Condition and Roof Age
- Roof age **MUST** be provided for all submissions.
- If roof age is **> 20 years**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- If roof shows active leaks or missing shingles, the risk **MUST BE DECLINED** unless repaired prior to inception.

### 2.2 Electrical / Plumbing
- Aluminum branch wiring **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- Galvanized plumbing in whole-house configurations **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- Federal Pacific (FPE) or Zinsco panels **MUST BE DECLINED** unless replaced.

## 3. Loss History
### 3.1 Claims Thresholds
- If claims count is **≥ 2 water losses within 36 months**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- If claims include **non-weather water damage** with no mitigation, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED**.

## 4. Catastrophe & Hazard Signals
### 4.1 Wildfire Hazard
- If wildfire risk score is **High**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED**.
- If wildfire risk score is **Severe**, the risk **MUST BE DECLINED** unless defensible space mitigation is documented (see §4.3).

### 4.2 Flood Hazard
- If the risk is in a **Special Flood Hazard Area (SFHA)**, the risk **SHALL BE REFERRED** and flood coverage requirements **MUST** be evaluated (see Endorsements §2.2).

### 4.3 Mitigation Requirements (Wildfire)
- For wildfire score **High** or **Severe**, defensible space evidence **MUST** be obtained:
  - brush clearance ≥ 100 feet where feasible
  - no combustible materials within 5 feet of structures
- If mitigation evidence is missing, the submission **MUST** enter the Missing-Info Loop and request documentation.

## 5. Decision Outcomes
### 5.1 Accept
- The risk **MAY** be accepted if no referral/decline triggers apply and required fields are present.

### 5.2 Refer
- The risk **SHALL BE REFERRED** if any “SHALL BE REFERRED” condition is met.
- Referral **MUST** include a summary of triggers and requested documentation.

### 5.3 Decline
- The risk **MUST BE DECLINED** if any “MUST BE DECLINED” condition is met.
