# Rating and Endorsement Guidelines (Synthetic)

> SYNTHETIC DATA. Fictional rating values for demonstration only.

## Section 5.1 — Base Rate

The indicative annual premium starts from a base rate of $4.50 per $1,000 of
Coverage A dwelling amount. All multiplicative factors below are applied to this
base premium to produce the premium indication.

## Section 5.2 — Construction Factors

- Fire-resistive: 0.85
- Masonry: 0.95
- Masonry veneer: 1.00
- Frame: 1.15
- Manufactured: 1.35

## Section 5.3 — Roof Covering Factors

- Metal: 0.92
- Tile: 0.95
- Asphalt shingle: 1.00
- Flat built-up: 1.10
- Wood shake: 1.40

## Section 5.4 — Roof Age Factors

- 5 years or less: 0.95
- 6 to 15 years: 1.00
- 16 to 25 years: 1.20
- over 25 years: 1.45
- unknown (documented default): 1.05

## Section 5.5 — Deductible Factors

- $500: 1.10
- $1,000: 1.00
- $2,500: 0.90
- $5,000: 0.82

## Section 5.6 — Wildfire Surcharge

Wildfire band factors: low 1.00, moderate 1.10, high 1.30, very_high 1.60. When
qualifying wildfire mitigation is documented, a 0.90 mitigation credit is applied
on top of the band factor.

## Section 5.7 — Incomplete Rating Inputs

If a required rating input is missing and no documented default exists, the
submission is routed to review rather than rated on assumptions. Only the
documented defaults in this section may be used to fill gaps.
