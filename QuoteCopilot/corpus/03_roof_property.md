# Roof and Property Condition Guidelines (Synthetic)

> SYNTHETIC DATA. Fictional guidelines for demonstration only.

## Section 3.1 — Roof Age Thresholds

Roof age is a primary driver of water and wind loss. The following bands apply:

- Acceptable: roof age 20 years or less is eligible without a roof-age referral.
- Referral (RC-202): roof age greater than 20 years and up to 30 years must be
  referred for a roof inspection and possible repair/replacement requirement.
- Knockout (RC-201): roof age greater than 30 years exceeds the maximum
  acceptable roof age and must be declined under reason code RC-201.

If roof age is not supplied, intake must request it before assessment; missing
roof age is a required follow-up fact.

## Section 3.2 — Roof Covering Type (RC-203)

Wood shake and wood shingle roofs present elevated ember and fire exposure and
must be referred under reason code RC-203 regardless of age. Asphalt shingle,
metal, tile, and flat built-up roofs do not trigger a covering-type referral on
their own.

## Section 3.3 — Construction Class

Construction class affects both eligibility and rating. Frame, masonry, masonry
veneer, and fire-resistive construction are eligible. Manufactured/mobile homes
are eligible only with documented tie-down and skirting; otherwise refer.

## Section 3.4 — Square Footage and Stories

Dwellings exceeding four stories are outside the HO3 program. Square footage is
used for valuation cross-checks against the requested dwelling amount.
